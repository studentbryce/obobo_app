How to Use

Stopwatch: Press "Start" to begin counting time. Press "Stop" to pause. Press "Reset" to set the time back to 00:00:00.00.

Lap Timer: Press "Start" to begin timing. Press "Lap" to record individual lap times. Press "Stop" to pause and "Reset" to clear all laps.

Alarm: Set the time using the time input and press "Set Alarm." When the alarm rings, it will blink "ALARM!" and play an alarm sound. Press "Cancel Alarm" to stop it.
